# Finnish Digital Observatory

Welcome to the Finnish Digital Observatory: an emerging research infrastructure for studying Finnish digital society, online communities, and platform dynamics.

Over time, we will

1. create and maintain software assets to organise data collection systematically, thus addressing methodological, ethical, and legal challenges in a holistic manner within the Finnish environment
1. build documentation and a knowledge base to develop competencies of researchers and support staff in digital trace data collection
1. organise systematic data collection for research purposes
